Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Airbnb listings in New York City using 2019 data. The interactive visualization explores pricing dynamics, neighborhood trends, host performance, and demand indicators across NYC boroughs. The dashboard enables potential hosts, travelers, and market analysts to make data-driven decisions through intuitive visual exploration.

Key Objectives:

  • Identify pricing patterns across different NYC neighborhoods
  • Analyze room type distribution and its impact on pricing
  • Discover top-performing hosts and their market strategies
  • Visualize geographic distribution of listings and prices
  • Understand availability and demand correlation

Dataset Information

The dataset used is AB_NYC_2019.csv from Inside Airbnb, containing detailed information about Airbnb listings in New York City as of 2019.

Dashboard Visualizations Created

Interactive Charts & Components:

  • Geographic Map - Listing distribution across NYC
  • Dual-Axis Chart - Stacked bar + line chart combination
  • Donut Chart - Room type or borough distribution
  • Top 10 Hosts Table - Most active hosts in NYC
  • Horizontal Stacked Bar Chart - Multi-category comparison
  • Bar Chart - Price distribution by category
  • Treemap - Hierarchical data visualization
  • Horizontal Bar Chart - Ranking visualization
  • Highlight Table - Heatmap-style data table
  • Text Sheets - Key metrics and KPIs
  • Interactive Filters - Borough, room type, price range
